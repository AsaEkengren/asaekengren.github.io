# Introduction
I played drums and composed with Idiacanthus, an instrumental, progressive rock project formed in Madrid, Spain, in 2018. We composed a set of songs for nearly three years, but the COVID-19 pandemic unfortunately resulted in the studio recording being cancelled and the group disbanding.

## Lineup
**Asa Ekengren** -:drum: Drums  
**Jaime Pascual** - :guitar: Bass  
**Ali** - :violin: Violin and :notes: vocals  
**Tomas** - :guitar: Guitar
